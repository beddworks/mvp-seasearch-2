### 1. SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT

Vertex Capital Partners Pte. Ltd. ("the Client") hereby engages SeaSearch Executive Recruitment Pte. Ltd. ("the Firm") to conduct an executive search for the following position(s):

- **Chief Financial Officer (CFO)** — Singapore HQ
- **Head of Portfolio Management** — Singapore HQ

This engagement is on a **contingency basis**. The Firm will present qualified candidates; no fee is payable unless and until the Client makes a formal offer of employment that is accepted by the candidate.

---

### 2. FEE STRUCTURE

#### 2.1 Placement Fee

The placement fee is calculated as **20% of the candidate's agreed first-year total cash compensation**, which includes:
- Base salary
- Guaranteed bonuses (if any)
- Sign-on bonus (if any)

Variable bonuses, equity grants, and long-term incentive plans are excluded from the fee calculation.

#### 2.2 Platform Fee

SeaSearch charges a platform administration and recruitment management fee of **20% of the gross placement reward**. This fee is separate from the placement fee and is payable directly to SeaSearch upon successful placement confirmation.

#### 2.3 Example Calculation

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Candidate first-year salary | SGD 300,000 |
| Guaranteed bonus (15%) | SGD 45,000 |
| Total compensation | SGD 345,000 |
| Placement fee (20%) | SGD 69,000 |
| SeaSearch platform fee (20% of placement fee) | SGD 13,800 |

---

### 3. PAYMENT TERMS

- **Invoice date:** Upon written confirmation of the candidate's acceptance of offer
- **Payment due:** Net 30 days from invoice date
- **Currency:** Singapore Dollars (SGD)
- **Bank transfer details:** To be provided on invoice

Late payments will incur interest at 1.5% per month on outstanding amounts.

---

### 4. GUARANTEE PERIOD

SeaSearch offers a **90-day replacement guarantee** from the candidate's first day of employment. Should the placed candidate resign or be terminated for cause within 90 days, SeaSearch will conduct one replacement search at no additional placement fee (platform fee applies to the replacement).

The guarantee is void if:
- The Client materially changes the role, compensation, or reporting structure
- The Client fails to pay the invoice within the agreed terms

---

### 5. EXCLUSIVITY

This engagement is on a **non-exclusive** basis. The Client retains the right to engage other search firms or conduct direct sourcing in parallel. However, candidates introduced by SeaSearch and subsequently hired within 12 months of introduction are subject to the fee in Section 2.

---

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding:
- Candidate identities and CVs submitted
- Client's strategic hiring plans and compensation ranges
- Any proprietary information shared in the course of this engagement

---

### 7. TERM

This agreement is valid for **12 months** from the date of signing and renews automatically unless terminated by either party with 30 days' written notice.
