### 1. SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT

NovaBridge Technologies Sdn. Bhd. ("the Client") engages SeaSearch Executive Recruitment Pte. Ltd. ("the Firm") for an executive search retainer for the following roles:

- **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** — Kuala Lumpur HQ
- **VP of Engineering** — Kuala Lumpur HQ

This engagement is structured on a **milestone retainer basis**. Fees are disbursed in staged payments tied to measurable delivery milestones, as detailed below.

---

### 2. FEE STRUCTURE — MILESTONE RETAINER

Unlike a standard contingency model, NovaBridge Technologies requires a structured milestone payment schedule to align incentives across the full search lifecycle.

#### 2.1 Milestone Schedule

| Milestone | Trigger | Amount (MYR) |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Milestone 1 — Engagement kick-off | Signed agreement + intake call completed | MYR 15,000 |
| Milestone 2 — Longlist delivery | Minimum 8 qualified candidates presented | MYR 20,000 |
| Milestone 3 — Shortlist delivery | Minimum 3 candidates advancing to final interview stage | MYR 25,000 |
| Milestone 4 — Placement confirmed | Candidate accepts written offer of employment | MYR 40,000 |
| **Total per role** | | **MYR 100,000** |

For two roles, the total engagement value is **MYR 200,000**.

#### 2.2 Platform Fee

SeaSearch charges a platform and account management fee of **20% of the total milestone payments received**. This is invoiced separately at the completion of each milestone.

#### 2.3 Milestone 1 is Non-Refundable

The kick-off fee (Milestone 1) is non-refundable once the intake call has been completed and the search has commenced.

Milestones 2–4 are not payable if the search is terminated by the Client before that milestone is triggered.

---

### 3. PAYMENT TERMS

- **Invoice issuance:** Within 3 business days of milestone completion
- **Payment due:** Net 14 days from invoice date
- **Currency:** Malaysian Ringgit (MYR)
- **Method:** Bank transfer to SeaSearch designated account

No additional search work will commence until the preceding milestone invoice is settled.

---

### 4. REPLACEMENT POLICY

Should a placed candidate leave within **60 days** of start date (voluntary resignation or termination for cause), SeaSearch will re-engage Milestones 2–4 at 50% of the original milestone rates. Milestone 1 and any previously paid milestones are not re-charged.

---

### 5. EXCLUSIVITY

This engagement is **exclusive** for a period of **90 days** from the signed date. The Client agrees not to engage other search firms or post the roles publicly during this exclusivity window without prior written consent from SeaSearch.

After 90 days, exclusivity lapses automatically unless renewed in writing.

---

### 6. CANDIDATE OWNERSHIP

Any candidate introduced by SeaSearch to the Client during this engagement — or within 18 months of the agreement end date — who is subsequently hired by the Client in any capacity shall be subject to the full Milestone 4 fee.

---

### 7. REPORTING & COMMUNICATION

- Weekly progress reports delivered every Friday
- Dedicated Slack channel for real-time candidate updates
- Bi-weekly video check-ins with the hiring manager

---

### 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep all candidate data, compensation benchmarks, and strategic hiring plans strictly confidential. SeaSearch complies with PDPA (Malaysia) and PDPA (Singapore) requirements for all candidate data handling.

---

### 9. TERM & TERMINATION

This agreement is valid for **6 months** from the signing date.

Either party may terminate with **14 days' written notice**. Milestones already triggered and invoiced remain payable in full upon termination.

---

### 10. GOVERNING LAW

This agreement is governed by the laws of **Malaysia**. Disputes shall be referred to the Kuala Lumpur Regional Centre for Arbitration (KLRCA).
