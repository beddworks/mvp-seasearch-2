📄 Job description

About the role
DBS Bank is seeking a transformative Chief HR Officer to lead the people strategy for one of Asia's most innovative financial institutions. The CHRO will serve as a key member of the Group Management Committee, partnering with the Group CEO and Board to shape organizational culture, talent strategy, and the future of work across 18 markets and 40,000 employees.
Key responsibilities
  • Set and execute the Group HR strategy aligned to DBS's 3-year transformation agenda
  • Lead culture evolution, employee engagement, and employer brand across Asia
  • Drive end-to-end HR digital transformation including HRIS and AI-driven talent tools
  • Own board-level succession planning and C-suite talent pipeline development
  • Oversee total rewards, compensation benchmarking, and executive remuneration
  • Partner with regional MDs to localize HR programs across SG, HK, CN, IN, ID
  • Lead a team of 300+ HR professionals across all markets
Compensation
Base salary SGD 260K–340K + performance bonus up to 40% + LTIP / equity participation + full executive benefits package.
